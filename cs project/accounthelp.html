Account Help

Instructions for login and Sign up

Logging In :

1) Navigate to Login page:
• Open your web browser and go to the website's login page. This is typically found on the homepage or under a "Login" link.

2) Enter your Credentials:
• Locate the fields for entering your username/email and password.
• Type in your registered email address or username in the appropriate field.
• Enter your password in the password field. Ensure that the password is entered correctly and that Caps Lock is turned off if applicable.

3) Click the Login Button:
• After entering your credentials, click the "Login" button.




Signing Up :

1) Access the Registration Page:
• Visit the website's homepage.
• Look for a "Sign Up," or "Create Account" link. Click on it to proceed to the registration page.

2) Provide Your Information:
• Fill out the registration form with the required information. This typically includes Full name, Email Address and password(create a strong password)

3) Agree to Terms and Conditions:
• Review the terms of service and privacy policy.
• Tick the checkbox to agree to these terms if you accept them.

4) Complete Registration:
• Click on the "Sign Up" Button to submit your registration information.
